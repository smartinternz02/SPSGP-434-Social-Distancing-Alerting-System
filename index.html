COVID-19 spread is emotionally challenging for many people around the globe, changing day-to-day life in unprecedented ways. All sections of society should play a vital role to protect themselves and each other and help prevent further spread of the disease. Social-distancing is an important way to slow down the spread of infectious diseases. People are asked to limit their interactions with each other, reducing the chances of the disease being spread with physical or close contact.
